About

This is our story!

  • 2009-2011

    Our Humble Beginning

    Thomas Smith and Ryan both graduated locally from Wilde Lake High School in Columbia, MD. We have local roots and we respect our comminity. Between 2009-2011, Thomas and Ryan search for their purpose in life by going away to school.

  • March 2011

    A Dream is Born

    Co-owner and Founder, Thomas Smith, who had been working in the photography business for a few years, recognized the lack of choices in the business. He dreamed of a better future for the portrait studio business. He sought to utilize today's best technology.

  • December 2016

    Alliance with a Like-Minded Individual

    Ryan, a friend of Thomas, began getting interested in creating a business. He was upset with the beauracracy of the corporate world. After much discussion and debate over the costs of Thomas' venture, Ryan decided to help his friend get this business off the ground.

  • July 2017

    Snap Photography LLC is Born

    In July 2017, Snap Photography officially became its own corporation, born and bred in Columbia, MD. Thomas and Ryan found the resources to make their dream a reality. Snap Photography has made it their quest to provide the cheapest, most convinient service around, without sacrificing quality.
